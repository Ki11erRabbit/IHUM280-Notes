MMO Qualities

  • a mediated form of human interaction
  • know that real people are around you in the game
  • self invention through the ability to recreate self
  • character creation
  • allows for diversity
  • direct loop of success
  • game is constantly running

How MMOs Keep players

  • daily login bonuses
  • constant generation of new content
  • reward rest systems to prevent you from playing constantly (sustainable play)

Issues with MMOS

  • time factor
  • game wants you to be the only game you play
  • play every day, week, month, and year
  • Fear of Missing Out (FOMO) since game is updated with new content
  • Sunk Cost Fallacy since playing for longer results in more value in subscription

What Happens when an MMO Ends?

  • due to unsutainable growth of server costs and new update costs
  • when the game shuts down it is gone
  • Starwars Galaxies shut down in 2011
  • an example of what do you do when you are at the end of the world?
