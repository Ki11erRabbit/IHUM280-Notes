Interplay Rules (using Portal)

  • you can place portals on certain surfaces
  • you can jump
  • right click places orange portals, left click places blue portals
  • momentum continues through portals
  • signs tell you what some things can do
  • 1 Portal → 2 Portals

Evaluative Rules (using Portal)

  • knocking off cameras (game complains but also get achievement)
  • pellets activate makes a noise and lights up floor to some door or other thing
  • exit room to win, enter elevator to win
  • death for not doing well
  • game overal doesn't really evaluate you at all
