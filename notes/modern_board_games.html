Eurogame Design Characteristics

  • Indirect player conflict
  • No player elimination
  • Less randomness or luck, and the ability to mitagate randomness
  • Designer of game listed on the game box cover
  • High-quality artwork and components
  • A theme that is fairly benign (perhaps irrelevant)
  • Shorter playtime
  • Complecity in gameplay not the rules
