Games and Sociality

  • Remote: overcomes space and distance limitations
  • Asynchronous: players don't need to play the game at the same time (24 hours to make a move in a chess game)
  • Prosocial Emotions: (gentle) seasing, mentoring, etc
  • Ambient sociablity: aware that others are around and playing but the player is not being forced to interact with them. You are not alone but you do not need to interact with others. Low stakes socialization, socialbility on your own terms. less direct, less real time socialization

Games and Sociality (the dark side)

  • Distance or Remote: can't play local game with same console. must have screen for each one. not designed to be played in one room
  • Anonymity and Avatars: smurfing, saying nasty things to people
  • Unwanted Sociality: people who are predators
