Characters

Player Types

  • Avatar: you to be the presant in the world
  • Actor: personality and background are pre-defined
  • Role-Playing: personality and background are pre-defined but the player can manipulate and dictate it.
  • Iconic: no choice, background and personality are pre-defined (if any) but player can't manipulate it or dictate it, very memorable like an image icon

Henry Jenkins & Video Game Stories

  • Evoked Narratives: story exists in a different story (borrowed story)
  • Enacted Narratives: you the player makes choice in the story, story is told to you as you play (original story) (most common)
  • Embedded Narratives: stories come out as you play the game, story already occured and you have to discover it (original story)
  • Emergent Narratives: story elements but no set story, stories occure from interaction from within the game (original story) (most common)

Examples of Types of Narratives

  • Evoked Narratives: Harry Potter games, other games based on other material
  • Enacted Narratives: The Legend of Zelda games, Undertale
  • Embedded Narratives: Dear Esther, Ace Attorney, Danganronpa, Mystery games
  • Emergent Narratives: Dear Esther, Minecraft, Fornite
